Travel to Fujiyama

I would visit this wonderful mountain and fight bears on my way to the top.

In order to fight real bears, I'd enjoy japanese national cuisine, so that it would be easier to gain strength. This would include

  1. miso soup
  2. okonomiyaki
  3. takoyaki

Right after, in order to relax and regain my strength back, I would be delighted to try Japanese onsen, preferably with monkeys swimming with me. True reconnection with the wild!
